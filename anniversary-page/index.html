Our Story:

Once upon a time there were two hard working hospital workers.
One a wise (cracking) and handsome LPTA...

The other was a beautiful nurse with a great smile and an amazing body...

After several months of workplace conversations (and casual stalking) the brave (and foolish) man asked the woman out, and she agreed to go out with the charming man.

The two began spending a lot of time together...

It wasn't long before they feel head over heals in love...

Their love grew as the months went on, spending more and more time together, and one day they went on a trip to the mountains...

And he asked her to marry him! (please tell me she said no)

SHE SAID YES!!!!

And, within a year, they were married.
